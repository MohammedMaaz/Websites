Campuses A total of four campuses have set up by MIA in different regions of the country serving the Nation in various fields Here is a breif overview of each of those:

Main Campus

Situated at the heart of Karachi city, the main campus of MIA is a class of it's own. The infrastructure iself is a self-speaking example of it's quality-an elegant and techie design with a color scheme provoking innovative thinking among the pupils. The unit conatains a fully-fledged professional enviornment to fullfill the creative and innovative thirst of it's pupils. The campus spreads on almost an area of 15 hecerates and comprises of 6 blocks, in which 4 belong to the corresponding 4 departments while the other 2 include an administrative and a student facilitaion block. Departmental buildings are all well maintained and equipped to meet and even compete the standards of international institutes. Each department has it's own well equipped and well furnished laboratories of computer and major subject. There are 5 canteens in total, 4 departmental and a big faculty canteen. All products of canteen are continously visualized and go through several hygene tests to avoid any health hazards for the consumers. Modernized digital library is also present for the students to endeavour knowledge through modernised ways. A traditional 3-story full length library with over 3000 books on almost 70 different topics is also present with a capacity of over 250 students at a time. A huge auditorium with a seating capacity of almost 1000 is situated at the center of the campus, having proper acoustics and lightning. The auditorium remains open the whole year for studetns to showcase their talents and exhibit their performances in the field of science and technology. There is one big stadium for cricket, and two general purpose small sports grounds. The whole campus is covered with security cameras installed after every 100 meters, while go-through gates at the entrance add to the security to make it as fool proof as possible. In short there is everything you would expect to find in a well renowed international institute, so no need of getting abroad and waste your money, just walk towards MIA! Take Campus Tour
