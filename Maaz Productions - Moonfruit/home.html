This is a site about inspirational and exciting GW-BASIC Programs and Games. New GW-BASIC programs are added time to time and the existing ones are updated and upgraded. This is a very inspirational and motivational site for secondary, matric, and inter level computer science students, who usually underestimates BASIC for it's possibilities. This site is also a best platform to learn the basics of Programming through GW-BASIC. You can leave a message in the discussion menu or contact me through my facebook page to discuss any thing or issue regarding BASIC.
